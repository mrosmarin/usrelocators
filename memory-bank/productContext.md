# Product Context

## Why This Exists
Relocation transitions are operationally and emotionally complex; the product supports both consumers and professionals through a structured journey with coordinated tools and alerts.

## Problems Solved
- Fragmented move lifecycle tracking between clients and professionals.
- Lack of consistent stage-aware guidance and support assets.
- Weak operational visibility for agents/coordinators on client risk and next actions.

## User Experience Intent
- Consumer experience should be guided, low-overwhelm, and stage-aware.
- Professional CRM should surface actionable status, alerts, and touchpoints.
- Journey model should communicate a full transition lifecycle, not only transaction milestones.

## MVP UX Constraints and Signals
- Consumer dashboard should keep primary actions limited (<=3 top-level actions in initial contexts).
- 12-stage lifecycle must be represented consistently with final-stage messaging:
  - `Living in the Home`
  - subtext: `Relationship -> Future Moves`
